
Subject manipulator: 3-DOF 3R Planar Arm Mounted on a Controllable Cart

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

REQUIRED to be in the same folder: driver* files, RRA* files, prob1* files, draw* files, dirkin.m

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

For Least Norm Pseudoinverse Solution:

    a. open and run "driver_leastnorm_pinverse_soln.m"
    b. simulation will run.
    c. after simulation runs, the plot will be displayed.
    Note: To view the video animation file, open "leastnorm-pinverse.mp4"

---------------------------------------------------------------------------


For Gradient Projection Solution:

    a. open and run "driver_gradientprojection_method.m"
    b. simulation will run.
    c. after simulation runs, the plot will be displayed.
    Note: To view the video animation file, open "gradient-projection.mp4"

---------------------------------------------------------------------------

To watch and play the pre-generated output videos:

    a. Go to 'output videos' folder
    b. Play the videos
